NYC Open Data: DOB Job Application Filings dataset

Home Info Analysis

The DOB Job Apllication Fillings , provided by the NYC Department of Buildings (DOB) through NYC Open Data, tracks construction-related filings across the city, including permits, alterations, and new building projects. This dataset offers valuable insights into urban development trends, compliance with safety regulations, and economic activity in the construction sector. Our website enhances accessibility to this data by providing searchable records, interactive maps, and visualizations—enabling developers, policymakers, and researchers to analyze construction patterns, monitor zoning compliance, and identify growth hotspots. By transforming raw data into user-friendly tools, the platform supports transparency and informed decision-making for NYC’s built environment.
